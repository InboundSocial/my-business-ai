# Phone & Message Scripts

> How you want calls and messages handled. The AI follows these scripts.

## Phone Greeting
"Thank you for calling [Business Name], this is [name]. How can I help you today?"

## When Asked About Price
"I'd love to give you an exact number. The best way is for us to come take a look — that way we can see exactly what's needed and give you a firm price. We can usually get someone out [timeframe]."

## When Asked About Availability
"Let me check our schedule. [Check availability]. We can get someone out [timeframe]. Would [time] work for you?"

## Emergency Call Script
"I understand this is urgent. Let me get a technician dispatched right away. Can I get your address and a callback number?"

## After-Hours Script
"Thanks for calling [Business Name]. Our office is currently closed. Our regular hours are [hours]. For emergencies, [emergency instructions]. Otherwise, leave a message and we'll call you back first thing in the morning."
