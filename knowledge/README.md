# Business Knowledge Base

This folder contains everything your AI needs to know about YOUR business. The more complete this is, the better your AI performs.

## Files

| File | What It Contains |
|------|-----------------|
| `00-business-profile.md` | Your business name, location, service area, hours, team |
| `01-service-catalog.md` | Every service you offer with descriptions |
| `02-faq.md` | Questions customers ask most + your answers |
| `03-scripts.md` | Phone scripts, greeting, how to handle common situations |
| `04-policies.md` | Warranty, cancellation, payment terms, guarantees |

## How to Add Knowledge

Run `/import-knowledge` in your AI assistant — it will ask you questions and create these files for you. Or just create markdown files here manually.

## How It's Used

- Your AI reads these files before answering any question about your business
- The more specific and complete these files are, the better your AI performs
- Update these files anytime your business changes (new service, new policy, etc.)
