# Bullet & Fascination Frameworks

Reference doc for writing sales bullets (aka "fascinations"). Load when writing
bullet lists for offer stacks, VSL slides, email teasers, or any copy that needs
high-density curiosity and desire in a single line.

Based on Jon Benson's Rich Bullet Formula and classic direct response patterns.

---

## Why Bullets Matter

Bullets are the hardest-working copy on any page. A great bullet:
- Creates a curiosity gap the reader MUST close
- Delivers a specific, believable benefit
- Sounds like a secret they're not supposed to know

Bad bullets list features. Great bullets sell the click, the scroll, the purchase.

---

## The Rich Bullet Formula

Every killer bullet has 5 parts:

```
(Lead) + (Verb) + (Hook) + (Turn) + (Close)
```

| Part | Job | Example Fragment |
|------|-----|------------------|
| Lead | Opens the loop | "The single most important TRUTH if you want to..." |
| Verb | Creates momentum | "...rapidly experience..." |
| Hook | Paints the payoff | "...a surge in energy, taking you back to your college years..." |
| Turn | Adds surprise/twist | "...The uncanny truth revealed..." |
| Close | Stacks more value | "...along with 3 nighttime energy boosters!" |

You don't need all 5 every time — but Lead + Hook + Turn is the minimum for a
bullet that stops the scroll.

## The 20 Core Bullet Patterns

### 1. How To + Warning
**Template:** How to `(verb)` `(topic)` using `(trick)`...just never `(warning)`!

**Example:**
- How to erase fine lines using the juice from 2 common fruits...just never DRINK IT!
- How to book 15 jobs a week using one Google setting...just never touch the budget cap!

**When to use:** Promise a specific result with a surprising caveat.

---

### 2. Why ___ Never Works
**Template:** Why `(common approach)` never works for `(topic)`...and what to do instead (hint: it involves `(trick)`)

**Example:**
- Why counting calories never works for lasting weight loss...and what to do instead (hint: it involves your left hand!)
- Why hiring more techs never fixes your capacity problem...and what to do instead (hint: it involves a $3 kitchen timer!)

**When to use:** Dismantle a misconception and tease the real answer.

---

### 3. The Fastest Way
**Template:** The fastest way to `(goal)` without `(fear)` or `(objection)`...do it `(ease benefit)`!

**Example:**
- The fastest way to fill next week's schedule without spending a dime on ads or begging for referrals (do it once and FORGET IT!)

**When to use:** Promise speed + remove the two biggest barriers.

---

### 4. The TRUTH About
**Template:** The TRUTH about `(topic)` and the `(villain)` that `(bad result)`...PLUS what's REALLY `(concern)`!

**Example:**
- The TRUTH about SEO and the underhanded agency tricks that burn through your budget...PLUS what's REALLY driving leads in 2025!

**When to use:** Expose a hidden enemy to build "us vs. them" authority.

---

### 5. (X) Tricks To
**Template:** `(Number)` tricks to `(goal)`...without `(ethical objection)`

**Example:**
- 5 tricks to close higher-ticket jobs...without pressure tactics or feeling sleazy!

**When to use:** Numbered list that feels low-friction and ethical.

---

### 6. How To (Benefit) WITHOUT (Pain)
**Template:** How to `(benefit)` without `(objection)` or `(unlikely)`...works `(counter-objection)`!

**Example:**
- How to grow revenue 40% without hiring a single person or working weekends (works even if you're in a small market!)

**When to use:** Highlight ease by removing every expected requirement.

---

### 7. Better Than ___!
**Template:** "Better than `(standard)`!" How this weird `(trick)` `(benefit)` as if you just `(standard experience)`!

**Example:**
- "Better than a $5K coach!" How this weird 1-page checklist gets your team running SOPs as if you just hired a franchise consultant!

**When to use:** Position against the known-but-expensive alternative.

---

### 8. How A Simple ___ Can (Benefit)
**Template:** How a `(ordinary object)` and nothing more can `(benefit)` in `(timeframe)`!

**Example:**
- How a simple Post-It note and nothing more can double your closing rate in 14 days!

**When to use:** Create curiosity through an absurdly ordinary object.

---

### 9. The (Branded Term) Trick
**Template:** The "`(Branded Name)` Trick" used by `(envy targets)` to `(benefit)` with `(reverse objection)`!

**Example:**
- The "Post-It Note Trick" used by the world's top CEOs to do the work of 3 people with energy to spare!
- The "Kitchen Table Close" used by 7-figure contractors to win $50K jobs in a single visit!

**When to use:** Proprietary name + high-status association = maximum intrigue.

---

### 10. Are You...?
**Template:** Are you and `(helper)` making these common `(fear)` mistakes when it comes to `(topic)`? Find out `(urgency)`!

**Example:**
- Are you and your marketing agency making these common lead-killing mistakes with your Google Ads? Find out before you spend another dollar!

**When to use:** Create immediate anxiety around something they're currently doing.

---

### 11. Did You Know
**Template:** Did you know there's a `(adjective)` `(method)` for `(goal)` that anyone can `(ease)`?

**Example:**
- Did you know there's a simple 3-question script for handling "I need to think about it" that closes 60% of stalls on the spot?

**When to use:** Introduce a little-known method with implied accessibility.

---

### 12. A Secret
**Template:** The secret of the `(branded name)`: `(benefit)` using only `(minimal input)`!

**Example:**
- The secret of the "2-minute morning review": eliminate 80% of daily fires using only your phone and a checklist!

**When to use:** Classic curiosity driver — works everywhere.

---

### 13. What To Never
**Template:** What to NEVER `(action)` (this common `(thing)` `(scary consequence)`)!

**Example:**
- What to NEVER say when a customer asks "Can you do it cheaper?" (this common reply costs contractors $47K/year!)

**When to use:** Warning-based curiosity that feels like insider knowledge.

---

### 14. Warning
**Template:** WARNING: `(common action)` `(scary consequence)`. Replace it with THIS `(benefit)`!

**Example:**
- WARNING: Sending proposals by email costs you 35% of your closes. Replace it with THIS 5-minute ritual and watch your win rate jump!

**When to use:** Urgent, high-stakes situations. Use sparingly.

---

### 15. Right? Wrong!
**Template:** `(Common belief)`, right? WRONG! You can `(surprising truth)` using `(method)`!

**Example:**
- Great copy takes years to learn, right? WRONG! You can write killer sales bullets in less than a day using 3 foundational formulas!

**When to use:** Shatter a limiting belief in a single line.

---

### 16. The #1 ___ For
**Template:** The #1 `(category)` for `(specific benefit)` FAST!

**Example:**
- The #1 follow-up sequence for turning dead leads into booked jobs FAST!

**When to use:** Simple authority claim. Best when backed by proof nearby.

---

### 17. How (Authority) Did (Goal)
**Template:** How `(known figure)` `(achieved goal)`...and why they attribute `(percentage)` of their success to `(one thing)`!

**Example:**
- How a $4M plumbing company cut callbacks by 70%...and why the owner attributes 90% of the fix to ONE morning meeting!

**When to use:** Borrow authority from a real or aspirational figure.

---

### 18. Where To
**Template:** Where to find `(valuable thing)` for `(surprisingly low cost)`...they'll even `(bonus)`!

**Example:**
- Where to find pre-qualified homeowners actively searching for your service...they'll even call YOU first!

**When to use:** Resource-finding bullets. Great for guide/toolkit offers.

---

### 19. Why You're Stuck
**Template:** Why you're `(negative state)` without even knowing it...and the `(simple fix)` that `(outcome)`!

**Example:**
- Why you're bleeding $3K/month in wasted ad spend without even knowing it...and the 10-minute audit that stops the leak!

**When to use:** Diagnose the root cause of failure. High empathy.

---

### 20. The Big Revelation
**Template:** The `(dramatic adjective)` discovery that forced `(person)` to `(dramatic action)`...and why it `(benefit)` for everyone who tries it!

**Example:**
- The gut-punch realization that forced a 20-year contractor to fire his office manager...and why his profit doubled in 90 days!

**When to use:** Story-driven curiosity. Best for VSL slides and email hooks.

## Micro-Lead Starters

These are plug-and-play openers you can attach to any bullet. Mix and match
with the patterns above.

### Curiosity & Intrigue
- A recently uncovered secret that lets you...
- A "sneaky" (I can't believe this works!) way to...
- A "lazy man's" technique for...
- 1 weird trick to...

### Numbered & Efficiency
- 2 QUICK tips to finally...
- 3 CLEVER ways to...
- 3 incredibly easy ways to...
- A near-instant way to...
- The quickest and easiest way to...
- The #1 EASIEST way to...

### Fear & Warning
- WARNING: "Never"...
- CAUTION: These "helpful" folks really want to...
- The 1 mistake even PROS make when...
- The Top 3 MISTAKES when it comes to...
- The #1 mistake most `(avatars)` make when trying to...
- What NEVER to...
- Why you must AVOID...

### Authority & Truth
- The TRUTH behind why...
- The single most important sentence you will ever read about...
- The single most important TRUTH if you want to...
- The BEST tip EVER if you want to...
- The absolute BEST/WORST time to...

### Question & Context
- Do you...?
- Should you...?
- Exactly where to...
- When it's safe to...
- Why it's SMART when you...

### Comparison
- BETTER than "____"! This works to...
- PLUS my complete list of tips to...
- PLUS: How to...

## Bullet Categories by Function

Use the right bullet type for the job:

| Category | Function | Best For |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Curiosity** | Opens a loop they must close | VSL slides, email subject lines |
| **Benefit** | Paints a specific outcome | Offer stacks, sales pages |
| **Fear/Warning** | Creates urgency through risk | Health, finance, compliance niches |
| **Authority** | Borrows credibility from others | Coaching, consulting offers |
| **Objection-Killer** | Preempts "yeah but..." | Mid-page, near CTA |
| **Proof** | Makes claims concrete and believable | Anywhere claims appear |
| **Story** | Micro-narrative in one line | Email hooks, VSL transitions |

## Quick Quality Check

Before shipping any bullet list:
- [ ] Does each bullet create a curiosity gap or paint a specific outcome?
- [ ] Would you click/keep reading just from the bullet alone?
- [ ] Are numbers specific (not round)? "$47K" not "thousands"
- [ ] Is there a mix of bullet types? (not all "How to..." or all "Warning...")
- [ ] Do the bullets sound like insider secrets, not marketing copy?
- [ ] Read them out loud — do any sound like a robot wrote them?
